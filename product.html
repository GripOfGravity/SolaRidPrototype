Sensors

The SolaRid device has a mutlitude of useful sensors. The sensors included with the machine are:

  • Insect Species
  • Soil Moisture
  • Leaf Temperature
  • Soil Temperature
  • Barometric Pressure
  • Precipitation
  • Wind
  • Insect Count
  • Ambient Temperature
  • Ambient Humidity
  • Radiation
  • Night Length
  • Lunar Cycle
