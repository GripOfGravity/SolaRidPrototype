Remote Data Survey and Collection

SolaRid assists research entomologists and crop scouts in their day to day duties by more effeciently harvesting insects for study or monitoring.

The data producing and collecting unit counts the number of insects harvested, and manipulates this data to give an accurate representation of the number of total potential insects in the fields.
